I describe my life, my music, my merchandise, and any ongoing tours on my website at https://iameden.eu/. I was born on December 23rd, 1995 in Dublin, Ireland. My music career started out on YouTube and soundcloud, under the aliases The Eden Project and The Spab project which were no longer used as of 2015. I went to college in Dublin, and graduated, going on to make my own record label "MCMXCV" and have released three albums since it's conception.
